Triangle Tracker

Types of Triangle are:

  1. Equilateral Triangle
  2. Iscocele Triangle
  3. Scalene Triangle
  • Equilateral is:
    • A Triangle that has all sides equal
  • Iscoceles is:
    • A Triangle in which two sides have the same length
  • Scalene is:
    • A Triangle that has three unequal sides
